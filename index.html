Biography

I am the founder of ChatExcel, a company at the Pre-A round. I am also a third year Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science at Peking University, advised by Prof. Li Yuan. I received my B.E. and M.S. degrees in computer science from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics in 2019 and 2022, respectively, advised by Prof. Sheng-Jun Huang. Moreover, I was a researcher in Tencent AI & ARC Lab and JD Business Growth BU for a year respectively. My research interests include machine learning and large language models.
